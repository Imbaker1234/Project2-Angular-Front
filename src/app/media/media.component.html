Art Media

A medium is the material used to create a work of art. Discover the myriad forms of media that interest you!

Architecture

Architecture is primarily concerned with the design and construction of buildings. Media in this category often involves:

  • glass
  • metal
  • stones
  • bricks
  • wood

Drawing

Drawing is a broad form of art that includes several different types of media. Drawing media may involve:

  • graphite (pencil)
  • crayon
  • watercolor
  • oils
  • pastels
  • pen and ink
  • charcoal
  • sand

Electronic

Digital art creation involves the use of digital media and software to create live models, fims, or even content used in magazines, brochures, websites. Digital media include:

  • Graphic art software
  • 3D Computer graphics and printing
  • Word processing and decktop publishing
  • Digital photography and cinematography
  • Digital painting
  • Computer programming and simulation

Painting

Media in painting refers to the type of paint used and the base to which it is applied. Samples of traditional paint media include:

  • ink
  • primer
  • stencil
  • watercolor
  • oil
  • fresco

Sculpture

Sculpting involves the shaping of a solid structire and textrued surface to create a 3-dimensional object Scuplting contains different types of media for carving and casting such as:

  • cement
  • clay
  • ceramics
  • plastic
  • wax
  • plaster
  • sand
  • styrofoam
