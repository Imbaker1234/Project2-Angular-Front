Art Media

An art medium commonly refers to the material used to create a work of art. A non-exhaustive list of art categories and their associated media are listed below:

Architecture

  • cement, concrete
  • glass
  • metal
  • stone, brick
  • wood

Ceramics

  • clay
  • porcelain
  • pottery

Drawing

  • chalk
  • graphite (pencil)
  • ink
  • pastel
  • pixel

Electronic

  • graphic art software, 3D computer graphics
  • word processors and desktop publishing
  • digital photography and cinematography
  • programming languages

Painting

  • acrylic
  • watercolor
  • ink
  • fresco
  • oil paint
